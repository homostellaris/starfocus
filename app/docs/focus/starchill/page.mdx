# Starchill

Starchill is an in-app game you can play to clear the asteroid field and move on to your wayfinder.

The app will seamlessly transition into a topdown shooter where you control your starship and use three different weapons on the asteroids.

- Archive
- Snooze
- Call reinforcements

When you're ready to conclude the game the actions you've flagged with your weaponry will be taken so you can advance to the wayfinder in style.
