## What is Starfocus?

Starfocus is a todo app that embraces your imperfect human nature. Instead of rigid scheduling and never-ending backlogs, we focus on:

- **Goal-setting** - Define what matters to you
- **Brain-dumping** - Capture ideas without pressure
- **Space exploration** - Discover new ways to be productive

## Features

### Star Roles & Constellations

Organize your tasks around roles and constellations that reflect your life.

### Flexible Task Management

No rigid deadlines. No guilt-inducing overdue tasks. Just thoughtful productivity.

### Self-Defined Productivity

You decide what productivity means for you.
