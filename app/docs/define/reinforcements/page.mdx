# Reinforcements

We will make it seamless to pull your friends and allies into Starfocus (with their very own starships of course) so that they can assist you with your objectives.

You will be able to delegate by email address, your allies will receive an email, and when they click on it they'll see their very own view of Starfocus with all objectives that have been shared with them so its as easy as possible for them to be a part of your journey.
